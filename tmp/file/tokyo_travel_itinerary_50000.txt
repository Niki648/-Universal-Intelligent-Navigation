# Tokyo Travel Itinerary (Budget: ¥50,000 RMB)

## Overview
A 7-day premium Tokyo travel experience including accommodation, transportation, dining, attractions, and shopping.

## Budget Breakdown (in RMB)
- Flights (round-trip): 8,000
- Accommodation (6 nights luxury hotel): 12,000
- Transportation (Suica card, taxis, airport transfers): 2,000
- Dining (breakfast, lunch, dinner daily): 10,000
- Attractions & Activities: 5,000
- Shopping & Souvenirs: 8,000
- Emergency fund: 5,000

## Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival at Narita Airport → Check-in at Shinjuku luxury hotel → Evening stroll in Kabukicho
Day 2: Meiji Shrine → Harajuku Takeshita Street → Shibuya Crossing → Shibuya Sky
Day 3: Tokyo Disneyland full-day experience
Day 4: Asakusa Senso-ji Temple → Tokyo Skytree → Sumida River cruise
Day 5: TeamLab Borderless digital art museum → Odaiba island exploration
Day 6: Day trip to Nikko → Traditional ryokan dinner
Day 7: Ginza shopping → Last-minute souvenir shopping → Departure

## Tips
- Best time to visit: March-April (cherry blossoms) or October-November (autumn foliage)
- Learn basic Japanese phrases
- Download Google Translate and Japan Travel apps
- Reserve popular restaurants in advance
