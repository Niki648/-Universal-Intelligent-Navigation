# Tokyo Travel Plan - Budget: ¥50,000 RMB

## Overview
A comprehensive 7-day Tokyo travel itinerary designed for a budget of ¥50,000 RMB (approximately ¥1,000,000 JPY).

## Itinerary Summary
- Duration: 7 days / 6 nights
- Best time to visit: March-April (cherry blossom season) or October-November (autumn foliage)
- Estimated total cost: ¥49,800 RMB

## Detailed Breakdown

### Flights (Round-trip)
- Economy class from major Chinese cities: ¥4,500–¥6,000 RMB
- Recommended airlines: ANA, JAL, or Peach Aviation

### Accommodation (6 nights)
- 3-star hotel in central Tokyo (Shinjuku/Shibuya): ¥3,200 RMB/night × 6 = ¥19,200 RMB
- Alternative: Business hotels or capsule hotels: ¥1,800 RMB/night × 6 = ¥10,800 RMB

### Transportation
- Airport transfer (Narita/Haneda → Tokyo): ¥200 RMB
- Suica/Pasmo card & recharge: ¥500 RMB
- Daily subway/bus: ¥300 RMB/day × 7 = ¥2,100 RMB
- Total: ¥2,800 RMB

### Food & Dining
- Breakfast: ¥150 RMB/day × 7 = ¥1,050 RMB
- Lunch: ¥250 RMB/day × 7 = ¥1,750 RMB
- Dinner: ¥400 RMB/day × 7 = ¥2,800 RMB
- Snacks & drinks: ¥1,000 RMB
- Total: ¥6,600 RMB

### Attractions & Activities
- Tokyo Disneyland/DisneySea: ¥800 RMB
- TeamLab Borderless: ¥400 RMB
- Tokyo Skytree: ¥300 RMB
- Meiji Shrine & Harajuku: Free
- Asakusa & Senso-ji Temple: Free
- Shibuya Crossing & shopping: Variable
- Total: ¥2,500 RMB

### Shopping & Souvenirs
- Budget: ¥5,000 RMB

### Miscellaneous
- Travel insurance: ¥300 RMB
- SIM card/WiFi rental: ¥400 RMB
- Emergency fund: ¥1,000 RMB
- Total: ¥1,700 RMB

## Total Estimated Cost: ¥49,800 RMB

## Money-Saving Tips
- Book flights & hotels 2–3 months ahead
- Use convenience store meals (7-Eleven, FamilyMart)
- Many temples, parks & neighborhoods are free to explore
- IC cards (Suica/Pasmo) simplify transport & small purchases

## Essential Japanese Phrases
- Hello: Konnichiwa
- Thank you: Arigatou gozaimasu
- Excuse me / Sorry: Sumimasen
- How much?: Ikura desu ka?
- Where is...?: ...wa doko desu ka?

## Important Notes
- Check visa requirements for your nationality
- Passport must be valid for ≥6 months
- Download offline maps & translation apps (Google Translate, Japan Travel)
- Respect local customs: quiet on trains, no eating while walking, proper shoe removal
