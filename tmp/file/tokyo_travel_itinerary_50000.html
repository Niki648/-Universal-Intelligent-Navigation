Tokyo Travel Itinerary (Budget: ¥50,000 RMB)

Overview

A 7-day premium Tokyo travel experience including accommodation, transportation, dining, attractions, and shopping.

Budget Breakdown (RMB)

  • Flights (round-trip): 8,000
  • Accommodation (6 nights, luxury hotel): 12,000
  • Transportation (Suica, taxis, transfers): 2,000
  • Dining (3 meals/day): 10,000
  • Attractions & Activities: 5,000
  • Shopping & Souvenirs: 8,000
  • Emergency fund: 5,000

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Narita Airport arrival → Shinjuku hotel check-in → Kabukicho evening walk
  2. Day 2: Meiji Shrine → Harajuku Takeshita Street → Shibuya Crossing → Shibuya Sky
  3. Day 3: Full day at Tokyo Disneyland
  4. Day 4: Asakusa Senso-ji Temple → Tokyo Skytree → Sumida River cruise
  5. Day 5: TeamLab Borderless → Odaiba island
  6. Day 6: Day trip to Nikko (UNESCO site) → Ryokan dinner
  7. Day 7: Ginza shopping → Souvenir pickup → Departure

Tips

  • Best seasons: March–April (cherry blossoms), October–November (autumn colors)
  • Download Google Translate and Japan Travel app
  • Book popular restaurants in advance
  • Get a Suica/Pasmo card upon arrival
